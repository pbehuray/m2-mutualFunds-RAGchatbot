## Selection: HDFC Mutual Fund

### Justification

1. **Comprehensive Documentation**: HDFC Mutual Fund provides excellent documentation including:
   - Detailed scheme information sheets
   - Key Information Memorandums (KIM)
   - Portfolio holdings
   - NAV history
   - Fact sheets with complete metrics

2. **Data Quality**: High-quality, structured data with consistent formatting across schemes

3. **Accessibility**: All documentation is publicly accessible without requiring login or registration

4. **Market Presence**: One of the largest and most trusted AMCs in India, ensuring data reliability

5. **Scheme Diversity**: Wide range of schemes across all categories (equity, debt, hybrid, solution-oriented)

6. **Reference Alignment**: Groww platform (reference context) has comprehensive HDFC scheme data, making it easier to validate and cross-reference

7. **Website Stability**: Reliable website with good uptime and consistent URL structure
